My Work


1.Responsive E-Commerce Website

This project focused on creating a responsive e-commerce website tailored for a client in the fashion industry. The website was designed and developed to ensure a seamless experience across various devices and screen sizes. Key features include a modern and sleek design, user-friendly navigation, and the implementation of a secure payment system. The ultimate goal was to deliver an online shopping platform that not only looks visually appealing but also provides an easy and secure shopping experience for users.

2.Mobile App for Task Management

In this project, I developed a mobile application dedicated to simplifying task management and collaboration. The app boasts real-time updates, task assignment capabilities, and progress tracking functionalities. The user interface was meticulously crafted to ensure a seamless experience on both iOS and Android platforms. This app is designed to cater to the needs of busy professionals and teams, enhancing efficiency and communication in task-oriented workflows.

3.Corporate Branding and Identity

For this project, I took the lead in a comprehensive branding initiative for a corporate client. The process involved creating a new logo, designing various marketing collateral, and establishing brand guidelines. The primary objective was to revitalize the brand with a fresh and modern identity while preserving its core values. The outcome is a cohesive and recognizable brand image maintained consistently across a variety of platforms, ensuring a strong and unified presence in the market.
